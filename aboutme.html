Let's Work

Hi there! I'm Grace.

I specialize in visual storytelling as a tool for problem-solving. My work focuses on translating complex ideas—whether strategic, cultural, or product-driven—into creative that feels clear, human, and memorable. I work across brand, marketing, and experiential design, shaping concepts, messaging, and visual systems that help ideas land with intention rather than excess.

I believe great design should simplify, connect, and inspire—never just decorate. My approach balances creativity with restraint, using visuals to guide understanding, build trust, and create meaning. If something doesn’t communicate or move the idea forward, I don’t believe it belongs.

When I’m not working, I’m usually at home with my cats — my unofficial creative directors and favorite chaos-makers :)
